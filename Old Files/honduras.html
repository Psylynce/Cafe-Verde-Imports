Founded in 2001, Café Organico Marcala S.A. is an
organization made up of 790 small coffee producers. COMSA is
located in the Montecillos region of the Southwest of Honduras and
works to offer the highest quality bean while aiding the economic
development of Honduras. COMSA is certified FLO-CERT Fair
Trade, organically certified by Dakks, USDA, as well as Small
Producers certified. Their primary emphasis is on sustainable
production and quality coffee.

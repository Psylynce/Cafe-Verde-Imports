Damota Wolayta farmers' Cooperative Union was established in 2003 in the Wolayta region of Ethiopia and is currently made up of 64 primary cooperatives. Of those, 42 are involved in coffee production and marketing. The coffee farmers of the Wolayta region continue to use the traditional methods of organic shade-grown coffee. They harness the potential of the high altitude, ample rainfall, fertile soil, and optimum temperature range of the Wolayta region of Ethiopia to produce the highest quality Arabica Altura coffee beans.

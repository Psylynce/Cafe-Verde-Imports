Kenya

Farm: Simar

Region: Kiambu

Altitude: 1700-2100 meters, 5500-6900 feet

Varietals: SL28

Harvest: October-March, June-August (fly crop)

Process: Washed and sun dried

Bag Size: 60kg, 132.28 lbs

Simar is a Norwegian company that strives for excellence in its coffee, fairness towards their farmers, and coffee industry development in Kenya. Which is why Simar actually roasts, processes and packs its own brand of coffee in the Kiambu region, in addition to exporting green beans. The Kiambu region is 1,700 meters above sea level in a beautiful and lush mountain area. The height makes the cherries struggle to mature which is essential to produce high quality coffee.

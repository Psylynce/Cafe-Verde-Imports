El Salvador

Farm: La Providencia

Region: La Libertad

Altitude: 1200-1750 meters, 4000-5700 feet

Varietals: Primarily Pacas and Pacamora

Harvest: October-March

Process: Washed and mechanically dried

Bag Size: 60kg, 132.28 lbs

Coffee production in El Salvador has fueled the Salvadoran economy and shaped its history for more than a century, providing more than 50% of the country's export revenues. El Salvador has all the right ingredients – soil, altitude, climate- to produce high quality Arabica Altura coffee beans. Over 90% of El Salvador's coffee is shade-grown and around 80% of El Salvador's forests are associated with shade coffee plantations.

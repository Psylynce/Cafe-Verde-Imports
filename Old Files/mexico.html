Region: Chiapas

Certifications: IMO-Control, FLO-CERT, Certimex, Chiapas Certificate

Altitude: 1200-2000 meters, 4000-6500 feet

Varietals: Catuai, Caturra, Maragogype, and Bourbon

Harvest: December-April

Process: Washed and sun dried on cement patios

Bag Size: 30kg, 66.14 lbs

E-Café S.A. is a corporation established by a Christian Mission School in the southernmost region of Mexico near the border of Guatemala. E-Café produces 100% organic coffee. In 2012 E-Café produced 1,000 tons of "Arabica Altura" beans and 50 tons of "Maragogype" beans, both grown at a minimum of 1,700 meters. E-café also works with several coffee communities in Chiapas, based on the principles of Fair Trade.

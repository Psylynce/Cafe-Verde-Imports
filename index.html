About Us

Café Verde Imports

Café Verde Imports started in the mountains of Nicaragua. Our founder, Michael Pearl, was traveling through Nicaragua as a volunteer on local coffee farms. Michael spent time living, working, and learning about the cultivation process and harvest process of green coffee beans. Through working with different farmers and different cooperatives, Michael found the highest quality green beans in the northern mountains of Estelí, Nicaragua.

Michael came back to the United States with an incredible product that needed to be shared with all coffee drinkers. In 2011 Michael founded Café Verde Imports, LLC. Café Verde Imports supplies coffee traders and roasters with green beans all over the world.

Sustainable Tourism

Café Verde Imports reinvests much its coffee trading proceeds into its “Sustainable Tourism” efforts. Our goal is to replenish the Nicaraguan economy by bringing tourists and volunteers to the beautiful country of Nicaragua.
